It took me a while to find the test! Goodness, so many test makers around and the author delivers in MS Word... http://www.businessballs.com/freematerialsinword/vaklearningstylesquesti...

I am 40% visual, 30% auditory and 30% kinesthetic according to the test. However, it has two general features that make me seriously question it.

First of all, it comes from pre-computer era. It made me realize how tremendously communication and learning changed in the last twenty years. "When I first contact a new person, I usually..." - write them an email or text chat, duh!

Second, the test seems to confuse "auditory" and "people-oriented." I scored 1 out of 10 on music intelligence and I am not an auditory learner by how I see myself, yet I selected many of the auditory tasks because they had to do with people, while other options were solitary. For example:
8. When I am choosing a holiday I usually:
a) read lots of brochures
b) listen to recommendations from friends
c) imagine what it would be like to be there
My "listening" takes form of reading online, mostly. It's the "friend" part that is important.
