Hey all!  Welcome to our little section of the great Intro to PHP course!

Here's some steps:

1. Make sure you're in my section by checking your username against this list:
http://p2pu.org/webcraft/node/13221/document/24157

2. Go to http://whenisgood.net/s9zhhjq and fill out meeting times that are good for you.  We're going to try to keep this time the same every week, so think about what works best for you on a regular basis.  Also be sure to double check your timezone!  
2a. When you fill out the schedule, use your P2PU username as your name so I know who's completed the schedule.
2b. In the comments, fill in any preferred communication channels (Skype, Google Chat/Talk, etc.).  We may try to use the Chat here in the course site, but it'll be good to know what else works.

3. I'm 95% sure I'm going to set up a Google Group for course asynchronous communications (it's worked well in the past), but in the meantime, feel free to say hello in the comments here!  Or ask any initial questions.
